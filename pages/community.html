About Vidor OS

Vidor OS is more than just a distribution. It's a carefully crafted system built specifically for users who demand power, simplicity, and control.

Built on Debian Bookworm - We removed the bloat and kept only what matters.

CLI-First Philosophy - Boot directly to terminal. Choose your desktop environment later if needed.

Hardware Optimized - Special support for WiFi adapters, ThinkPad devices, and modern systems.

Customizable Setup - Customize your system exactly as you need it with a CLI-first approach.
